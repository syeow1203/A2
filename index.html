ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN YEAR 2023

Economic development is the transition of underdeveloped, low-income national economies into advanced industrial economies. While it is occasionally used interchangeably with economic growth, it typically refers to a transformation in a country's economy that encompasses both qualitative and quantitative enhancements. It aimed at enhancing living standards, reducing poverty and fostering sustainable progress. It encompasses policies and strategies that promote economic growth, job creation, and better access to education, healthcare, and essential services, ultimately contributing to an improved quality of life for a nation's citizens.
