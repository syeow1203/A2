CONSUMER PRICE INDEX IN YEAR 2023

The Consumer Price Index (CPI) serves as a crucial economic indicator in the realm of economic development. It is primarily utilized as the most widespread measure of inflation, offering insights into the effectiveness of government policies. Moreover, the CPI plays a pivotal role as a deflator of other economic series, allowing adjustments for price changes and facilitating the translation of these series into inflation-adjusted figures. Business executives, labor leaders, and private citizens rely on the CPI as a guiding tool when making pivotal economic decisions.
